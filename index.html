Voice Experience

Speech synthesis built to sound unmistakably natural.

Aurelia's voice engine runs on a low-latency synthesis pipeline tuned for natural pacing, intonation, and clarity — across every character and every call.

  • Realistic Speech Synthesis

    Voice models trained to reproduce natural rhythm, emphasis, and breath, not flat machine cadence.

  • Natural Pacing

Personalization

It remembers what you've told it — that's the whole feature.

Aurelia's memory system stores details from previous conversations — names, preferences, ongoing topics — and brings them back into future conversations, so interactions build on what came before instead of starting over.

See it in the app

Stored Details

Contextual Recall

You're In Control

Memory is a data feature: it stores information you've shared and retrieves it when relevant. It does not reflect awareness, feeling, or understanding on the part of the AI.

Questions

Good to know before you start.

An AI Companion is a software character you can talk or speak with inside the app. It's powered by language and voice generation models, and it's built for conversation, entertainment, and creative roleplay. It is not a person, and it doesn't have feelings, awareness, or a life outside the app.

The app stores details from your past conversations — names, preferences, ongoing topics — and references them in later conversations, so things feel continuous instead of resetting every session. You can view, edit, or delete what's stored at any time from Settings → Memory Controls.

Yes. Studio-tier accounts can build a character from scratch — appearance, personality traits, voice style, and conversational tendencies are all adjustable. Free and Premium accounts can choose from the existing character gallery and adjust personality settings within each one.

Voice calls use real-time speech synthesis: your speech is transcribed, processed, and answered with generated audio in the chosen character's voice style. Calls are included on Premium and Studio plans, with sub-second response latency in most network conditions.

Conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't sell conversation data to advertisers or third parties. You can export or permanently delete your data, including stored memory, from Settings → Data & Privacy at any time.

No. Every character is AI-generated — the artwork, the voice, and the conversation are all produced by software. None of the characters are based on real individuals, and none of them possess genuine thoughts, emotions, or awareness.
